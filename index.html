A quick explaination of our game

Attack On Castle is a 2 player co-op Tower-Defense game, with simple but enjoyable mechanics, it’s main attrait is it’s camera based on RTS gameplay. The goal of the players is to defend the castle using multiple defensive buildings from medieval times and to fight wave of enemies. The ultimate goal of the game is to finish the entirety of the content. The game is thought to be highly replayable thanks to the exponential difficulty.
